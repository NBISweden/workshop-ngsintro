Umeå city buses:
There are several options to pay your bus ticket, including on the bus (credit card only) or in advance either online or using the app, or in the ticket machines at Vasaplan or Umeå Airport. It is cheaper to buy the ticket in advance. More information can be found at Ultra’s homepage. The bus stop in the city centre is Vasaplan and the stops near the course venue are either Universum (buses 2, 5, 8 or 9) or Samhällsvetarhuset (bus 1). The trip takes about 6-7 min.

Airport bus:
The airport bus (Bus 80) goes from the airport to Vasaplan (the city centre), and takes about 8 min. The travel planner can be used to plan your trip.
